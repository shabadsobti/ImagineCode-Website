Our Mission

The long-term vision of ImagineCode is to create a next generation learning platform that reaches out and provides knowledge to the most underprivileged regions in the world. Computer science is the language of technology. This language describes the structures, processes, and relationships of the complex technological systems that shape modern society. Currently, the technology world revolves mostly around developed countries in which kids have access to a plethora of learning tools at a young age. We aspire to be part of a movement that pushes for the development of impoverished countries through STEM empowerment. We want the world to be one with more equality of opportunities for everyone, where anyone is allowed to strive for greatness regardless of their socio-economic condition.
