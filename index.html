What is ImagineCode?

ImagineCode seeks to empower children in technologically impaired classrooms to use coding as a tool to develop solutions to existing problems in their communities. Our platform is a decentralized, off the grid class-room in a box aimed at introducing children to the basics of programming and electrical engineering

It brings all the resources to a resource limited classroom. Using our platform, a deskilled curriculum can be run by a facilitator, and reduce the need for expert teachers. It is targeted specifically at developing nations, and aims to put the learning into context in rural areas
